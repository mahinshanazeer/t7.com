About Us

Trailblazing Adventures: Uniting Passion for RC, Mountain Biking, and Motorcycling in Kerala

We are a group of seven individuals who have embarked on a collective journey through life. United by a shared passion for RC cars and mountain biking, our story began in 2017, a time when radio-operated auto models were still a novelty in Kerala. At that point, only a handful were acquainted with these pursuits. As time progressed, so did our camaraderie, culminating in the formation of a community in 2018. Our mutual devotion to RC, mountain biking, and motorcycling served as the cornerstone of our bond. One of our noteworthy achievements was the successful organization of Polygon Bicycles, Game of Thrones 2021, Chapter 2 in Ernakulam, in collaboration with 'Old Spoke House, Aluva.' It's worth noting that the majority of our members, though employed across various sectors, possess remarkable expertise in these hobbies. We proudly represent Kerala in national RC race competitions. Presently, we orchestrate a wide array of events, including RC car expos, RC car racing, MTB racing, and Motorcycle group rides spanning the entirety of Kerala. Over the past two years, we've conducted engaging RC events in more than 45 colleges. Our journey is ever-evolving, with new horizons awaiting us.
